About Me

Creative & Versatile
Professional

I am a creative and versatile professional with over 8 years of experience in photography, media production, customer service, and agribusiness support. I am highly skilled at managing clients, delivering high-quality visual content, and providing exceptional service in fast-paced industries.

I hold a B.Sc. in Statistics from the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta. My diverse background — from editing high-quality photos and short films, to customer-facing roles where I completed over 3,000 successful rides as a driver — has taught me multitasking, problem-solving, and adapting to diverse environments.

Currently, as a Freelance Photographer and Picture Editor, I have provided photography and editing services for weddings, corporate events, and fashion brands with over 100 satisfied clients.
